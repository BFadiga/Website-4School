About

We want to bring you the ultimate platform to review and rate your favorite (and most hated) games.

  1. To provide a clean, easy to use tool.
  2. Provide community-based forums for all questions surrounding games.
  3. Rating system that is complex yet simple enough to match your carefully formed opinion; a 1-10 rating system, including decimals!
  4. Pleasant user interface experience that will (we hope) keep you coming back to rate your games whenever you feel like it!
  5. A new home for you to easily access and discover all current trending games, providing you with extra perspective from other users reviews.
  6. Intricate system built to let you easily search games by Genre, Rating and Developer/Publisher.
  7. Provide the user with the information required to play whatever game they might be interested in: minimum and recommended requirements for Computers.
  8. An interface that can easily provide the user with information on whether there are any discounts on key sellers or major gaming platforms.
